Project Engineer

Solutions Recruitment

Working for an established engineering company based in Tyne and Wear as a Project Engineer. Responsible for the project administration, commercial management and engineering of a contract. Activities include contract review, design review, overseeing of conceptual and detail design work, planning, procurement of the more complex items of hardware, financial control of allocated budgets, document control, and all client liaison. The Department is also responsible for the compilation of tender documentation. Provide project management support to the Project Manager in all project administrative activities. Ideally, the successful candidate will have/be A strong Project Engineering career background. Degree qualified, preferably Mechanical Engineering or other related discipline. Need a broad theoretical and practical knowledge relating to varied aspects of mechanical design and manufacture. Offshore Oil and Gas contracting experience would be beneficial but not essential. Experience of computerised systems is essential
